ICONS/SLOGENS PROS AND CONS OF USING WEBSITE Pros of Using a Website as a Barber 1. 24/7 Online Presence • Clients can find your services anytime, even outside business hours. 2. Appointment Booking • Online booking systems reduce missed calls and scheduling conflicts. • Customers like the convenience of booking without needing to call. 3. Professional Image • A clean, modern website builds trust and makes your business look more credible and established. 4. Marketing & Visibility • Shows up in Google searches, helping new clients discover you. • You can showcase reviews, promotions, or special offers. 5. Portfolio Display • You can upload photos of haircuts, beard trims, and styles to show your skills. 6. Customer Communication • Add FAQs, pricing, directions, and contact details so customers don’t need to call for basic info. 7. Integration with Social Media • Link your Instagram, TikTok, or Facebook to drive traffic between platforms. Cons of Using a Website as a Barber 1. Initial Setup Costs • Buying a domain, hosting, and getting a good design can cost money. 2. Maintenance • You need to update prices, promotions, or schedules regularly. • Outdated websites can turn customers away. 3. Competition • Many barbers rely on social media only (like Instagram), so standing out online may take extra effort. 4. Technical Skills Required • If you’re not tech-savvy, you might need to hire someone to manage the site. 5. Time-Consuming • Building content, uploading photos, and handling inquiries can take time away from cutting hair. 6.
